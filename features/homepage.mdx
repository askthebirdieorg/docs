### Available features

1. **Pending Leave Requests**:

   * If you are a manager, you’ll see notifications for leave requests that require your approval.

2. **Upcoming Holidays**:

   * View public holidays or custom holidays set for your organization.

3. **Today’s Leaves**:

   * See a list of employees who are on leave today.

4. **Upcoming Birthdays**:

   * A quick glance at team members’ birthdays coming up soon.

### Why It’s Important

> The home page centralizes critical updates, making it easier to stay informed and manage your organization efficiently.
