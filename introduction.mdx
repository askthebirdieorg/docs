This is the documentation for **Askthebirdie**, an open-source leave and time management platform designed to simplify how teams manage time off. Whether you're setting up for the first time or looking to integrate with tools like Slack, Askthebirdie gives you everything you need to track and organize leave policies seamlessly.

Here, you’ll find comprehensive guides covering every aspect of the platform — from setting up your organization and configuring policies to managing team members and using our integrations. Whether you’re an admin configuring the system or a team member requesting leave, this documentation provides step-by-step instructions to help you get the most out of Askthebirdie.

For developers, Askthebirdie’s open-source nature means you can extend and customize the platform to meet your specific needs. While the API documentation is coming soon, this space will eventually include advanced guides for integration and development.

Explore, learn, and make the most of Askthebirdie for your organization!
